Privacy Policy

Welcome to Chefi, a digital cooking course platform that connects professional chefs with users who want to learn cooking online.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights users have regarding their personal information.

1. General

By using the Chefi website or platform, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our services.

In this policy:

  • “Platform” means the Chefi website and any related digital services.
  • “User” means any person who visits, registers, purchases a course, or uses the platform.
  • “Chef” means a user who creates, uploads, or manages cooking courses on the platform.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

Information Provided by Users

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Profile details
  • Purchased courses
  • Course progress
  • Reviews and ratings
  • Content uploaded by chefs
  • Customer support inquiries

Technical Information

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Operating system
  • Login times
  • Pages viewed
  • Courses viewed
  • User activity on the platform

Payment Information

Payments may be processed through third-party payment providers. Chefi does not store full credit card details. Payment information is handled by the relevant payment provider according to its own privacy and security policies.

3. How We Use the Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Allow secure login
  • Provide access to purchased courses
  • Track learning progress
  • Display personalized course recommendations
  • Improve the user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Prevent fraud or misuse
  • Send system updates or marketing messages, where permitted

4. Sharing Information with Third Parties

Chefi does not sell users’ personal information.

We may share limited information with third-party service providers when necessary to operate the platform, including:

  • Hosting providers
  • Payment processors
  • Email service providers
  • Analytics tools
  • Customer support tools
  • Security and fraud prevention services

Information is shared only to the extent necessary to provide and improve our services.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Chefi may use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep users logged in
  • Improve website performance
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Measure marketing campaigns

Users may block or delete cookies through their browser settings, but some platform features may not function properly as a result.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, maintain security, resolve disputes, and manage user accounts.

Users may request account deletion, subject to any legal or business requirements that may require us to retain certain information.

7. Data Security

Chefi takes reasonable measures to protect user information, including:

  • Password encryption
  • Access control
  • Secure servers
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

However, no digital system is completely secure, and Chefi cannot guarantee absolute protection against unauthorized access.

8. User Rights

Subject to applicable law, users may request to:

  • Correct inaccurate information

  • Stop receiving marketing communications
  • Receive information about how their data is used
